How to Use This LDA Topic Visualization

  • Each circle represents a topic discovered by the LDA algorithm.
  • Click on a topic to see its most relevant keywords on the right panel.
  • The size of each circle shows how prevalent that topic is in the data.
  • Hover over keywords to see their importance within the selected topic.
  • You can zoom and pan the plot if your browser supports it.

Tip: If the visualization does not display, try refreshing the page or opening it in a different browser.
